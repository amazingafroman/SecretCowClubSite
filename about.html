about

Founded in late 2016 in a very not so secret space, a game jam. By 5 very tired and confused creative's who spent 48 hours attempting the impossible. Make a 3D third-person stealth game about a cow pretending to be human...

It was a failure... However, the 5 bonded that weekend, over dumb memes and failure the club was born.

From then the club has continued to make games in short time frames. They grew in size to 6 people and all the while still not learning from past mistakes, they continue to over scope but somehow they still deliver the “goods”.

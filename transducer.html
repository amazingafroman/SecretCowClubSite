transducer

Global Game Jam 2018

Locked away in a bunker deep down in the NSA basement, the Unidentified Transmissions and Frequency Description (UTFD) spend countless hours decrypting mindless chitter chatter from signals of unknown origin.
It's a very important job (So your told).

 

Created in 48 hours for Global Game Jam 2018, for the theme transmission. Transducer is a puzzle game where players must tune waves to the match the incoming transmission.

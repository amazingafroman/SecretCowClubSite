yassss queen

Global Game Jam 2017

The people adore you, their queen. When you make an appearance they come in droves to see you, to get the chance to be waved at by you, their queen. So give them what they want, QUEEN.

YASSSS QUEEN is a simple VR Game designed to not accurately simulate being a queen. Your job as the queen is to find important people in the crowd and wave you little queeny heart out at them. If you miss one then it's game over! Keep on juggling the people's need for your attention.

Be their YASSSS QUEEN!
